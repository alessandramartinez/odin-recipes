Japanese Curry

Description

Japanese Curry is a popular dish containing some protein chuck roast or chicken, onion, potatoe, carrots and japanese curry. It is commonly served with rice and pickled radish.

This dish is served warm and tastes better the next day.

Ingredients

  • Chuck roast or chicken
  • Onion
  • Potatoe
  • Carrots
  • Beef stock/broth or chicken stock
  • Curry cubes

Instructions

  1. Sear and cook the meat
  2. Cook the vegetables
  3. Add the liquid and cubes
  4. Simmer all the ingredients together
  5. Serve with white rice and pickled radish
