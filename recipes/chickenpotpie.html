Chicken Pot Pie

Description

Chicken Pot Pie is a classic american dish that consists of chicken, carrots, green beans, and corn mixed together in a gravy. This mixture then gets topped with a biscuit like pie crust and baked. Most ingredients can be made or stored bought which makes the dish great in a time crunch.

This dish is traditionally served straight from the oven.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Onions
  • All-purpose flour
  • Salt and pepper
  • Milk and chicken broth
  • Chicken
  • Frozen mixed veggies
  • Refrigerated Pillsbury Pie Crust

Instructions

  1. Prepare the crust
  2. Make the sauce
  3. Stir the chicken and veggies
  4. Fill, Cover and Bake
