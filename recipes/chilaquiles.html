Chilaquiles

Description

Chilaquiles is very popular dish from Mexico. Chilaquiles can be made in a multitude of ways but traditionally the dish consists of tortillas, salsa, and queso.

But Chilaquiles is a very customizable dish. In today's recipes we will also include sour cream, avocado, and finally garnish the dish with a bit of cilantro.

Ingredients

  • Corn tortillas
  • Jarred salsa or homemade salsa
  • Cooking oil
  • Garnishes

Instructions

  1. Fry the tortillas
  2. Make the salsa (for today's we will use salsa rojas but it can also be made with salsa verde)
  3. Cook the salsa
  4. Coat the chips with the salsa
  5. Add garnishes
